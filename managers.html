Discover the best managers and formations for each playing style in eFootball

Xabi Alonso

Quick Counter Specialist

High pressing, fast transitions, and aggressive attacking play characterize Xabi's style.

Quick Counter: 88
4-3-3

Quick Counter Tactics

The Quick Counter playstyle focuses on rapid transitions from defense to attack, exploiting spaces left by the opponent. Players need high speed, acceleration, and stamina to execute this effectively.

Key Player Requirements
Speed 85+ Acceleration 85+ Stamina 80+ Offensive Awareness 80+ Ball Control 75+

Recommended Players

CF: Kylian Mbappé

Speed: 99, Acceleration: 97, Finishing: 90

LW: Vinícius Júnior

Speed: 95, Dribbling: 90, Acceleration: 94

CM: Jude Bellingham

Stamina: 88, Passing: 86, Ball Control: 87

Tactical Instructions

  • Use good defensive players.
  • Attacking style is "Fast Build-up"
  • Fullbacks should be "Defensive Fullback".
  • Wingers should "Cut Inside".
  • Striker should have "Anchoring" instruction

Mikel Arteta

Long Ball Counter Expert

Defensive solidity with quick transitions to target forwards using long passes.

Long Ball Counter: 88 Possession Game: 72
4-2-2-2

Long Ball Counter Tactics

This playstyle focuses on defensive organization and quick transitions using long balls to target forwards. Requires physically strong players with good positioning and heading ability.

Key Player Requirements
Physical Contact 80+ Heading 80+ Long Balls 80+ Defensive Awareness 80+ Stamina 80+

Recommended Players

CF: Erling Haaland

Physical: 94, Heading: 88, Finishing: 92

AM: Kaka

Ball Control: 93, Passing: 91, Dribbling: 90

CB: Virgil van Dijk

Defensive Awareness: 92, Heading: 90, Physical: 89

Tactical Instructions

  • Quick transitions using long passes after winning possession
  • Short build-ups and, focuses on direct play to forwards
  • Mid Block defense with a compact shape to absorb pressure
  • Flexible positioning allows fluid movement during attacks
  • Best with fast strikers and accurate long-pass players

Frank Rijkaard

Possession Game Master

Focus on ball retention, positional play, and creating numerical superiority.

Possession Game: 88 Long Ball Counter: 88
4-1-2-3

Possession Game Tactics

The Possession Game playstyle focuses on maintaining control of the ball, patient build-up play, and creating chances through positional superiority. Requires players with excellent technical skills.

Key Player Requirements
Ball Control 85+ Passing 85+ Dribbling 80+ Vision 80+ Offensive Awareness 80+

Recommended Players

CF: Lionel Messi

Dribbling: 97, Ball Control: 96, Passing: 90

AMF: Kaka

Passing: 93, Dribbling: 90+, Kicking Power: 90

DMF: Rodri

Passing: 86, Defensive Awareness: 88, Physical: 86

Tactical Instructions

  • Short, controlled passes to maintain possession
  • Players stay close for passing options
  • High pressing to recover the ball quickly
  • Emphasis on slow buildup and movement
  • Requires technical, high passing players
