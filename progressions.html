Player Progression Guide
Understanding Player Development

In eFootball, player progression is crucial for building a competitive team. Each player has unique growth potential based on their age, position, and current attributes.

Progression Levels Explained
1
Basic Development

Focus on fundamental attributes and balanced growth.

2
Intermediate Training

Position-specific training begins with moderate intensity.

3
Advanced Specialization

High-intensity training for key position attributes.

4
Elite Performance

Maximizing potential with specialized training programs.
